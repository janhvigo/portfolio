Process Mining: Domestic Dashboard in Celonis

Digital Business and Workplace Technology

In this project, we were tasked with creating a domestic process mining dashboard in Celonis. The instructor provided us with the international KPIs and benchmarks. Our responsibility was to design the domestic dashboard, analyze process performance, and identify key differences in throughput times, case duration, and workload distribution.

Celonis Dashboard Construction

We built an interactive Celonis dashboard focused on the domestic process. The dashboard included:

  • Case throughput times from submission to payment
  • Workload analysis by organizational roles
  • Identification of cases exceeding one year
  • Comparison against international benchmarks provided by the instructor

By highlighting these KPIs, the dashboard allows managers to detect bottlenecks, assign responsibilities more effectively, and optimize domestic process efficiency in line with international standards.
