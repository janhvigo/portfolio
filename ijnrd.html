Publishing Research: Generational Dynamics in the Marketing Funnel

In August 2023, I had the opportunity to publish a co-authored research paper titled ‚ÄúGenerational Dynamics in the Marketing Funnel‚Äù in the International Journal of Novel Research and Development (IJNRD)

This research explored how different generations interact with the different stages of a modern marketing funnel: Awareness, Consideration, Conversion, and Loyalty. Our goal was to identify generational patterns that could help marketers tailor their strategies more effectively. One of the key findings was that loyalty tends to increase with age, while younger generations display greater variability in the awareness and consideration stages.

The project involved survey-based data collection, statistical correlation analysis, and interpretation of consumer behavior through a generational lens. It was a hands-on experience in applying marketing theory, data analysis, and academic writing in a structured and publishable format.

The paper was accepted by IJNRD and published in Volume 8, Issue 8 (August 2023). This experience not only strengthened my research and writing skills but also gave me a clearer understanding of how theory translates into practical business insights.
